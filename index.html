Ethos

We are gum - a studio of diverse designers with various skillsets who want to explore different areas of design through a variety of projects, anywhere from conceptual to commercial.

We aim to provide a flexible workspace that will expand your knowledge and stretch your imaginations. We value each designer as an individual and want to build your confidence by encouraging self-expression and experimentation. We want every one of our sessions to be beneficial and most importantly, fun!

Come and get stuck in, if you want something you can really sink your teeth into.
